Frequently Asked Questions

What paper do you use?

All prints are made on Hahnemühle Photo Rag 308gsm, a museum-quality, 100% cotton paper with a smooth matte finish. This archival paper is rated to last over 100 years.

Are prints signed?

Yes, all limited edition prints are hand-signed and numbered by the artist on the front border.

How are prints shipped?

Prints are shipped flat in rigid mailers with acid-free tissue paper. Large prints are shipped rolled in sturdy tubes. All shipments are insured.

What is your return policy?

Due to the limited edition nature of these prints, all sales are final. However, if your print arrives damaged, we will replace it at no cost.
