Frequently Asked Questions

What paper and process do you use?

These are original handmade darkroom prints. Shot on film, hand-developed, and printed by the artist in the darkroom on FUJI DPII Matte fine art photographic paper.

Are prints signed?

Yes, all limited edition prints are hand-signed and numbered by Michael on the front border.

How are prints shipped?

Prints are shipped flat in rigid mailers with acid-free tissue paper. Large prints are shipped rolled in sturdy tubes. All shipments are insured.

What is your return policy?

Due to the limited edition nature of these prints, all sales are final. However, if your print arrives damaged, we will replace it at no cost.
