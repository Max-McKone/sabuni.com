About

Michael Sabuni

Michael Sabuni is a wildlife and documentary photographer whose work captures the raw beauty of nature and authentic human moments. Based between Kenya and the UK, his photography bridges cultures and landscapes, telling stories that resonate across borders.

With a deep connection to the East African landscape and its communities, Michael's work has been featured internationally, bringing attention to conservation efforts and cultural preservation. His distinctive approach combines technical precision with an intuitive understanding of his subjects.

His recent exhibition, Shuka & Soil, showcased the Maasai way of life through intimate 35mm film portraits and landscapes, celebrating themes of community, land, and simplicity.
