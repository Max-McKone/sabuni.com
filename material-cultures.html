Community & Sustainability

Exploring the intersection of community-led conservation and sustainable practices through visual documentation.

The Project

This project documents community-led conservation efforts and sustainable practices, exploring the relationship between material culture and environmental stewardship.

Documented across multiple locations, the series captures the tactile nature of sustainable construction and the community spirit behind these ecological initiatives.
